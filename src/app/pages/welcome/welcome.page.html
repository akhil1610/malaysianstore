Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I need a Whatsapp-integrated store?

In the age of social media, if you are not promoting your products on Facebook and Whatsapp you are missing out. Zero helps you to set up a Whatsapp-integrated shop, so you can sell products over whatsapp.

Is Zero free?

Yes, Zero is on a mission to help small scale businesses by providing them free services. Taking orders, enquiries and receiving payments will always be free. You just have to pay a minimal amount to increase your product limit.

How do I take payments?

You can use your existing Bank account. Zero offers integrations to take payments via different methods like Credit, Debit, eWallet etc.

Can I use my own domain name?

Yes you can connect your own personal domain very easily with Zero . You get a free domain connection with Zero s Yearly Package.
